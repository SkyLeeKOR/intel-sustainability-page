Building a More Sustainable Future

Our RISE strategy guides our mission. We're committed to being Responsible in our actions, building an Inclusive culture, achieving world-class Sustainability, and Enabling our partners through technology to create a better world for all.

2030: Achieve Net Positive Water

On hover, you'll see more details.

We will conserve 60 billion gallons of water and restore more freshwater than we consume. Our goal is to fund water restoration projects that benefit local communities and watersheds.

2030: 100% Renewable Energy

On hover, you'll see more details.

We commit to powering our global manufacturing operations with 100% renewable electricity. This involves investing in green power sources and improving our energy efficiency across all facilities.

2040: Net-Zero Greenhouse Gas

On hover, you'll see more details.

Our goal is to achieve net-zero greenhouse gas emissions across our direct operations (Scope 1 and 2). We will continue to innovate in sustainable manufacturing and invest in carbon removal technologies.

2050: Zero Waste to Landfills

On hover, you'll see more details.

We aim to achieve zero total waste to landfills. This involves designing products for circularity, increasing recycling rates, and partnering with our supply chain to create a more sustainable product lifecycle.
